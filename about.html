Nowadays the primacy of vehicle-based mobility has become a widespread problem in most cities all over the world. However ,In Australia ,the car has been an even more necessary role than most European countries.

Car sharing is one of a number of mobility strategies which solves some car ownership problems and problems associated with high- car use demand. By this service, people who need a car from time to time rent vehicles rather than owning a vehicle. The research indicates more and more people think car share service is cheaper, cost effective and more convenient than owning a car i.e. not garaging or needing parking.

The system provides high security for the user who drives the sharing car. Users must be registered with their valid driving license and real name, address, cell phone number . This project also exemplifies Australian conditions as a basis on which to encourage local initiatives.
